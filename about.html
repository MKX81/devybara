About Thiago

Thiago Sena Dantas is a creative technologist currently enrolled in Hyper Island's XR Creative program (since 2024), where he's exploring immersive storytelling through VR, AR and mixed-reality experiences. Before pivoting to XR, Thiago built a strong foundation as a professional chemist, where he honed his analytical thinking, attention to detail and problem-solving skills in laboratory and process environments. Today, he leverages that scientific rigor to design and prototype engaging, user-centered XR applications—bridging his background in chemistry with a passion for cutting-edge interactive media.
